About Us

We are a small team of developers from Holberton school at Aguadilla, Puerto Rico. We are passionate about space and technology and we decided to create a simulation about our solar system that could help us explore and understand the wonders that we have outside our planet.

Our Planet

As big and infinte as the universe is there is still no proof to this date that there is life on other planets, our planet is the only one in our solar system with a habitable atmosphere.

Our Moon

We all love to see the moon in the night sky but believe it or not, you are looking at something that is 238,855 miles away from us. To put that into perspective that is as far as all planets in our solar sistem align together, crazy huh. The moon is the only natural satellite of the Earth.
